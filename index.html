What is needed?

No special equipment is needed for the first time. You can work out in long exercise pants and a t-shirt. We train all the techniques on both the right and left sides for a smooth workout of the body. A great focus is on training techniques, but the workouts should also be physically challenging. Aikido

The art was developed by Morihei Ueshiba in 1922. The movements consist of a variety of circular movements and spirals. It is important not to block the force of the attack, but instead to harmonize with the movement and pass on the opponent's power. Unlike many other martial arts and sports, Aikido has no competition moments.
